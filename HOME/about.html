THE CEO.

"

Hello, I’m Gracy Bawuza, CEO of Gracy'Kids Store. As a parent and entrepreneur, I believe childhood should be filled with comfort, creativity, and play. That’s why I started this kids store—a place where families can find clothes that let kids move freely and toys that spark imagination.


My Mission: To create a store that feels like a trusted friend to parents, offering safe, high-quality, and affordable products for children of all ages.

My Inspiration:Watching kids laugh, explore, and grow reminds me daily why this work matters. Every outfit and toy we choose is designed to support those magical moments.

My Promise: I personally ensure that our collections meet the highest standards of safety, durability, and fun. Because when it comes to kids, only the best will do.


At Gracy's Kids Store, we’re more than a shop—we’re a community. I’m proud to lead a team that shares my passion for making childhood brighter, one toy and one outfit at a time.
